Blackfort Wallet — Call Prep

Internal checkride · 30 min
Goal: align on the technical fit, identify the buying committee, walk out with a POV scope on Cosigner.
Treasury
~$10M EVM, expanding to Solana
Custody
Fireblocks
DEX protocol
Gnosis Safe (separate team)
Approval cycle
~2 days (CISO + Compliance)
Risk appetite
Considering memecoins
Competition
Hexagate (Chainalysis)
Principle for the call: the discovery questions force them to enumerate their defenses out loud. They will run out of layers before they reach the one only Blockaid provides. Don't pitch — let the silence do the work. Education moments come after they've felt the gap.
Sequence
1
Discovery · ByBit walkthrough

"If a ByBit-style attack hit you tomorrow — your Fireblocks UI is compromised, signers approve what looks routine but it's actually an owner change — walk me through every layer that catches it before signing."

Listen for
They'll start with Fireblocks policies and CISO review. Let them. Notice when they run out of layers — nothing simulates the transaction at the moment of co-signing. That silence is where Cosigner sells itself.
2a
Discovery · 2-day process

"Walk me through the last transaction that took the full 2 days. What were the CISO and compliance actually checking, and what tools did they use?"

Listen for
The review is mostly judgment, not informed verification. Probably reading raw calldata, trusting descriptions, pattern-matching. This is the augmentation pitch — Cosigner gives them automated verification so the human review focuses on the 2% that's actually risky.
2b
Discovery · Solana scale

"When you go live on Solana, transaction frequency is going to spike. How does the 2-day process scale — and what changes if it doesn't?"

Listen for
It doesn't scale. They know it. This makes Cosigner operationally required, not optional. Also surfaces Solana go-live timeline — get the date. Solana is our biggest wedge against Hexagate.
3
Bridge → Education · Safe / DEX

Bridge: "If the Safe front-end your DEX team uses got MITM'd tomorrow, what catches the bad transaction before signing?"

Then pivot to education
"To be clear — we're not replacing Safe's UI. We sit alongside it. Every transaction gets simulated and verified independently, so even if the front-end lies, the signer sees the truth."
Listen for
The DEX team rep — different buyer, different motion. Get their name and follow up separately. Note any specific incident that made them raise the Safe UI fear.
4
Bridge → Education · Memecoins

Bridge: "When the team allocates to a memecoin, who decides the token is safe to hold? What signals do they look at?"

Then pivot to education
"Token scanning runs at the cosigner moment — honeypot patterns, mint and freeze authority, holder concentration, contract risk. The swap doesn't go through if the token fails the scan."
Listen for
Usually nobody owns this. Don't moralize about memecoin strategy — frame as risk mitigation if they're going to do it anyway. Connects naturally to Solana (memecoin chain).
5
Discovery · Hexagate wedge

"Hexagate is solid for monitoring after the fact. But at the moment your CISO is staring at a raw transaction in Fireblocks deciding whether to approve — what does Hexagate show her?"

Listen for
They won't have a clean answer. Hexagate is monitoring-first; they don't sit in the cosigning flow. Push for parallel POV: "Run us both, compare what each catches." We win on simulation depth, token scanning, wallet-flow signals, and Solana coverage.
6
Discovery · Decision process

"If we walk out of this evaluation aligned on the technical fit — who else needs to be in the room before this gets signed, and what's their criteria?"

Listen for
Maps the buying committee. Identifies the economic buyer. Avoids the "great POV, six months of silence" trap. Treasury, DEX, and wallet may be three different buyers — find out now.

Walk-out checklist

  • Solana go-live timeline confirmed
  • Buying committee names captured (CISO, compliance, DEX lead, wallet product lead)
  • Hexagate stage understood (pre-POV, mid-POV, near-signature)
  • Fireblocks workspace setup and tx volume scoped
  • Agreed next step: Cosigner POV scope, with Safe + wallet as fast-follows
If they push back
"We already have Hexagate"
"Great — keep them. We solve a different problem. They watch your contracts; we sit in the signing flow. Run us in parallel during your Solana rollout and compare what each catches."
"Our CISO won't accept removing the human review"
"We're not removing it — we're giving her better information. Auto-approve the routine 80%, flag the 2% that needs her attention. Throughput goes up, blind-signing risk goes down."
"We're a wallet company — what do you do for our users?"
Acknowledge, then refocus: "Two separate conversations — happy to bring our wallet product lead in for that one. Today let's stay on the treasury and protocol side, since that's the ByBit risk you raised."
